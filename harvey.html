Christopher Harvey Mercado

Art is all around us, as we all know. I am not a particularly creative person, but I always do my best to make all of my projects outstanding. I had no notion what the subject of Contemporary Arts would teach us when I initially started; all I knew was that we would be making a lot of artworks. However, as time passes, my understanding of art continues to grow. Contemporary arts teach me a lot, make me more creative, and allow me to express myself more through art.

"Kinetic Art"

April 19, 2022 
The art that I made is a Kinetic art, and it is called “infinity cube”. It is called infinity cube because it never ends, you keep on folding and folding cubes. Iwas planning to put pictures on this cube, including all the people that makes me happy. My family, friends, favorite influencers, and more. But sadly, our printer got damaged and won’t print anything. That’s why I have decided that instead of pictures, I am going to put my favorite colors instead This is the art that I made because making this “infinity cube” makes me happy. Also, I putted 2 of my favorite colors, Yellow and blue. Also, it is fun at the same time, satisfying to use this, and every-time I use this “infinity cube” it makes me so happy.
