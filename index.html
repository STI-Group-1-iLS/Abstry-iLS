Who we are?

This website is a collection of ABSTRY's artist — the ABSTRYST — one-of-a-kind and beautiful artworks. It displays a variety of designs, sketches, and drawings created by the artist using his or her knowledge, perspectives, and imagination. This could be beneficial to persons who participate in activities that are related to the creation and demonstration of art. Arts can be expressed in a variety of ways, so come on in and let's go exploring!
