Daniela Mae Medina

Contemporary art is today's modern art. One of the things I've learnt while participating in those many activities is the importance of freedom of expression. I can convey my emotions while doing art. Because I can easily relate to the art I'm doing, I see myself in it. It allows me to express myself as well as understand my strengths and flaws. I can see the world clearly when doing someone else's art and applying it to myself. I am able to communicate with the art through various expressions and unique emotions. In this way, I learn more about myself and grow as a person.
