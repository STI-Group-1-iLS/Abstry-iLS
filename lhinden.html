Lhinden Shein Mandocdoc

I enjoy art but I am not very skilled at it. I enjoy drawing, painting landscapes, and creating DIY art, but the outcomes were not really impressive. I enjoy the thrill, satisfaction, and enjoyment that it provides. Contemporary art aids in the realization of these emotions. This course allows me to learn new things, expand my knowledge, and push myself to my limits. I'll admit that I'm not very good at this, but I enjoy trying new things. For me, art encompasses not only drawing, painting, participating in art challenges, and performing arts, but also the means by which I express myself and the pleasure I derive from doing so. This is the most exquisite work of art that a person can create.   

Lindenallee

 MARCH 2, 2022 
Linden tree is where my name comes from. I always compare myself to a linden tree because of its healing properties. Many common illnesses like cough, cold, and headache can be treated using different parts of this tree. I want to be as useful as this tree. I want to be someone’s healing and protection. I want to see those bunch of graceful trees with heart-shaped leaves, fragrant flowers and a small globular fruit hanging from a narrow leafy bract. I want to see those Avenue of Linden Trees at least once in a lifetime.

Happiness

April 20, 2022 
Happiness is a state of mind. Unlike some who describe happiness as if they were sitting in a cloud, I frequently envision and visualize myself in a field of flowers, enjoying the breeze and listening to the chirping of birds. Having no problems to think, simply appreciating nature and sitting in tranquility. This is what happiness feels like to me. It's not about having millions of cash, a lot of expensive items, or a lot of fans; it's about having peace of mind. It's not about owning anything, but about appreciating each and every aspect of it.

True Colors

The piece is titled "True Colors." On the video, you can see how the colors fade to black and white before returning to their original color. That is how others perceive me. At first, I appear to be cold, unfriendly, someone you should not mess with, scary, someone who is always angry, and hard-hearted. People frequently mistake me for that. It bothers me at times to realize that I am like that from someone else's perspective. But to be honest, I'm not that type of person. I am a more friendly person than you can imagine, easy to approach, cheerful, and adorable. I don't want to scare people; instead, I want to make them happy. Being mistaken for a cold person is fine, but being appreciated for your cold and warm phases is extremely amazing.
