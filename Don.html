Don Earl Lorenzo

Conart has been enjoyable for me because it allows me to explore my artistic ideas, but I'm not sure why I can't display it in my activities. It's as if I have brilliant ideas but don't know how to get them out. My time at Conart has been tough because, first and foremost, I can't draw and I'm not the type of person who invests a lot of time and effort in things like art. I don't have the opportunity to study the arts, but thanks to Conart, I am able to learn and grow. Conart has allowed me to learn more about myself in terms of my artistic side.
